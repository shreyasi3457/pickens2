🐾 About Biscuit

Biscuit came to us as a stray in the spring, found wandering near Pickens city park with the happiest tail wag you've ever seen on a lost dog. Despite everything, he was friendly to every person and every dog he met — which tells you a lot about who he is.

He's completed basic training and knows sit, stay, shake, and "gentle." He walks well on a leash once he burns off his initial excitement. He loves playing fetch, riding in cars, and sneaking onto the couch when he thinks you're not looking. He adores children and has been tested with dogs of all sizes.

Biscuit is ready for his forever home. He'll be a loyal shadow, a goofball companion, and your most enthusiastic alarm clock. He deserves a family that will love him as unconditionally as he will love them.

🏥 Health & Care

Biscuit has been fully vetted through our shelter program. He is up to date on all vaccinations (DHPP, bordetella, rabies), has been tested for heartworm (negative), is on monthly preventatives, has been microchipped, and is neutered. He is healthy and ready to go home.
